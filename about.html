About Me

A little introduction

I am Chiamaka Eke Love, a dedicated Technical Support Engineer at Tek Experts, a leading solution provider for Microsoft. With a passion for technology and helping others, I excel in my role, providing top-notch support to clients. As a friendly and approachable individual, I thrive in team environments and enjoy building strong relationships with colleagues and clients alike.

I completed my undergraduate studies in Computer Science at the University of Benin, graduating in 2019. Subsequently, I pursued a master’s degree in information technology from the University of Lagos in the year 2022.

I originate from the Aboh-Mbaise Local Government Area in Imo State. My birth and upbringing occurred in Lagos State, within a Christian household where I was nurtured in the Christian faith

My favorite quote; “Success is not the key to happiness. Happiness is the key to success. If you love what you are doing, you will be successful” _ Albert Schweitzer
